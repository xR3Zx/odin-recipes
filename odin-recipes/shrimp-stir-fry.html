Shrimp Stir Fry

This shrimp stir fry recipe with frozen vegetables, frozen shrimp, and a simple stir fry sauce â€” comes together in less than 30 minutes for a quick and easy dinner! Serve over rice or with noodles, and enjoy.


Ingredients:

  • 1 cup chicken stock
  • 1 cup frozen shrimp, thawed
  • 1 cup frozen mixed vegetables
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon butter, melted

Steps:

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. In a large skillet, melt butter over medium heat. Add garlic powder, black pepper, red pepper flakes, and salt; stir to combine.
  3. Add thawed shrimp and cook until pink and opaque, about 3-4 minutes.
  4. Add frozen mixed vegetables and chicken stock; bring to a simmer.
  5. Cook until vegetables are heated through and sauce has thickened slightly, about 5-7 minutes.
  6. Serve over rice or noodles.
  7. Enjoy your meal!
